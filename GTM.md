# Go-To-Market & Growth Strategy

## Landing Page Conversion Strategy
- **Hero Section:** Focus on the pain point: "You're overpaying for AI."
- **Social Proof:** Show a realistic fake audit showing $12,000/year savings.
- **Low Friction:** Don't require signup to generate the initial audit report.

## Product-Led Growth (PLG)
- **Shareable Reports:** The `/report/:id` route is designed to be shared with CFOs or Engineering Managers.
- **Lead Capture:** Gated AI Summary on the shareable report page captures emails for sales follow-up.

## SaaS Onboarding Flow
- Keep the audit form simple. Use intelligent defaults for plans and prices so the user doesn't have to manually look up what they are paying.
