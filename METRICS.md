# Frontend Product Metrics

## Key Performance Indicators
1. **Audit Completion Rate:** Percentage of users who land on `/audit` and successfully submit the form. (Target: >60%)
2. **CTA Conversion Rate:** Percentage of landing page visitors who click "Start Free Audit".
3. **Share Rate:** How often the "Copy Link" button is clicked on the `/report/:id` page.
4. **Lead Capture Conversion:** Percentage of report viewers who enter their email to unlock the deep-dive AI Summary.

## Tracking Tools
- Plan to implement Vercel Web Analytics for privacy-friendly tracking.
- Custom event tracking for "Tool Added" and "Report Generated" to understand user friction points in the form.
